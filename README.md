Room Inspiration Decor Hub

Room Decor Inspiration Hub is a platform where students can share and explore room decor ideas and inspirations, as living in small spaces and managing them can be challenging for many people.

Users can register and log in, and authenticated users can create, read, update, and delete inspiration posts.

Users can post pictures of their rooms or pictures of items that are functional and aesthetic at the same time.

Users will be able to browse and filter posts based on these tags, making it easier to discover ideas that match their personal style or needs.
